About Me

I am an undergraduate student in Product Design at Central South University, interested in human-computer interaction, human-AI interaction, multimodal interaction, user research, social computing, and computer graphics.

My work sits between design research and AI-assisted interactive systems. I care about how people understand, coordinate with, and build trust around intelligent tools, especially in creative workflows, healthcare decision support, and family-facing interactive systems.
